DIY AUDI

2003 A4 1.8T Quattro Tiptronic

Turbo Install

Turbo:After my meager stock k03 turbo started making loud whining noises I opted for a full custom large turbo swap. The stock turbo had a massive amount of turbine shaft play, and was blowing a healthy amount of oil into the air intake system. After a lot of research I choose a T3/T4 50 trim turbo, which makes out to be an excellent match for my engine and goals. It utalizes a journal bearing, so I won't have to worry about the common 1.8t engine killer... low rpm boost spikes. All together this turbo will be more than capable of my goal of 400 crank hp. To attach the new addition to the engine I purchased a durable cast iron manifold. The new manifold also flows much better than the tiny stock manifold. It came with a ported adapter for an external wastegate, but I blocked it off so I can use the new turbos internal wastegate.



Result:It took about a month for me to complete the build, but after all the new hardware was intergrated everything was worth the effort. I ended up fabricating my own turbo downpipe and about half of the exhaust system. I also fabricated a completely new intake system, oil feed and return lines, wastegate flange, and turbo-intercooler pipe. After getting the ECM tuned for the larger turbo the car feels amazing. I hit full boost at about 4000 rpm (18psi for now) and it pulls nicely.
